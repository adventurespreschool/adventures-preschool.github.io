# IQ4K Level 2 Certified Program

At Adventures Preschool, we are proud to be an Iowa Quality for Kids (IQ4K) Level 2 certified program. This certification demonstrates our commitment to providing high-quality early childhood education and continuously improving our practices to better serve the children and families in our community.

## What is IQ4K?

Iowa Quality for Kids is Iowa's Quality Rating and Improvement System (QRIS) for licensed child care centers and preschools. IQ4K provides five levels of quality that programs can achieve, with each level building upon the previous one. Through continuous quality improvement, we assess our practices and create structured plans to enhance the overall quality of our program.
