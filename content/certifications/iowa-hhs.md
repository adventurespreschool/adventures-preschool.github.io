# Iowa HHS Licensed Child Care Program

At Adventures Preschool, we are proud to be licensed by the Iowa Department of Health and Human Services (HHS). This license demonstrates our commitment to meeting and exceeding the state's rigorous health, safety, and educational standards for child care programs.

## What Does HHS Licensing Mean?

Iowa HHS licensing ensures that child care programs maintain high standards of care and comply with state regulations designed to protect children's health, safety, and wellbeing. Licensed centers receive at least one unannounced monitoring visit annually from HHS consultants, and our license is renewed every two years to ensure continued compliance with all requirements.

## Our Commitment to Safety & Quality

As a licensed center, we meet comprehensive standards across all aspects of our program:

### Health & Safety
Our program maintains strict protocols:
- All staff complete health examinations and background checks
- Safe building design with proper exits and fire safety measures
- Regular health and safety inspections
- Proper supervision ratios at all times
- Well-maintained indoor and outdoor spaces

### Staff Qualifications
Our educators meet rigorous standards:
- All staff complete required First Aid, CPR, and Mandatory Child Abuse Reporter Training
- Training in infection disease prevention
- Medication administration certification
- Ongoing professional development requirements

### Program Requirements
We maintain comprehensive documentation:
- Written policies and procedures
- Detailed emergency and evacuation plans
- Parent communication and orientation processes
- Child records and health documentation
- Regular program evaluations and improvements

### Physical Environment
Our facility meets all state requirements:
- Adequate indoor space (minimum 35 square feet per child) and outdoor space (minimum 50 square feet per child)
- Safe, age-appropriate equipment
- Proper lighting, ventilation, and temperature control
- Secure storage for medications and hazardous materials
- First aid supplies readily accessible
- Regular playground inspections and maintenance

### Nutrition Standards
We provide healthy meals and snacks:
- Staff trained in food safety and proper food handling
- Nutritious meal planning

### Accountability & Transparency
Families can trust our program:
- Open communication with licensing consultants
- Compliance with all state regulations
- Public access to licensing inspection reports
- Responsive to parent concerns and questions
- Commitment to continuous improvement

## Child Care Assistance (CCA) Provider

We are proud to accept Child Care Assistance, making quality early childhood education accessible to more families in our community. Child Care Assistance is available to income-eligible parents who are working, attending school, or participating in approved training programs.

### How CCA Works

If you qualify for Child Care Assistance:
- CCA is available for children under age 13 (or under 19 for children with special needs)
- Payment goes directly from the state to our program
- Families pay a co-payment based on income and family size
- We handle all billing and coordination with HHS

### Applying for CCA

Interested families can:
- Apply online through the Iowa Family Portal
- Contact your local HHS office for assistance
- Ask our staff for information and guidance
- Learn more at [hhs.iowa.gov/programs/programs-and-services/child-care/CCA](https://hhs.iowa.gov/programs/programs-and-services/child-care/CCA)

We're here to help answer questions about the CCA program and support families through the application process.

## Higher Quality, Higher Value

Programs participating in Iowa's Quality Rating System (IQ4K) are eligible for higher Child Care Assistance reimbursement rates. Our IQ4K Level 2 certification demonstrates our commitment to continuous quality improvement and enhanced standards of care.

## Regular Oversight & Inspection

Our program undergoes:
- Annual unannounced inspections by HHS licensing consultants
- Fire safety inspections by the State Fire Marshal
- Ongoing monitoring of compliance with all licensing standards
- Review of policies, procedures, and documentation
- Immunization Records verified by the Iowa Department of Public Health

These regular inspections ensure we maintain the highest standards of safety and quality for your child.

## Transparency & Communication

We believe families have the right to know about our program's quality and compliance. We encourage you to:
- Ask questions during tours or at any time
- Review our policies and procedures
- Request information about our licensing status
- Share concerns or feedback with us or directly with HHS

Our license is displayed prominently in our facility, and we're proud to share our commitment to excellence with every family we serve.

## Questions About Our License?

We're happy to discuss what our HHS licensing means for your family and how we maintain the highest standards of care. Feel free to ask during tours or contact us anytime.
